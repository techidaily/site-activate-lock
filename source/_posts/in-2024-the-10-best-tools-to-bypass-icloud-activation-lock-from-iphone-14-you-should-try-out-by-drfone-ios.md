To remove the iCloud lock, reset your Apple Watch Or Apple iPhone 14. Please note that this will erase all data on your watch. Here's how:

1. On your Apple Watch Or Apple iPhone 14, go to 'Settings'.
2. Tap 'General,' then 'Reset

5. Tap 'Erase All' to confirm.
